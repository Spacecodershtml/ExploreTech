Facts About Technology

Technology is advancing rapidly, shaping our lives in unexpected ways. Here are some interesting facts:

  • The first email was sent in 1971.
  • Over 90% of the world’s data has been created in the last two years.
  • 3D printing is being used to create everything from food to human organs.

History

The history of technology can be traced to the earliest tools that were made by humans, for example the stone tools from the stone age which were the early techniques that were used in an effort to change the environment of technology. For a millennia, societies have developed technologies that have majorly altered the societies, for instance the writing is an example of change. The Industrial Revolution that started in the 18th and 19th century led to the evolution of machinery and production systems, thus changing the face of economies and people’s lifestyles. In the 20th and 21st centuries, digital technologies like computers and the internet have revolutionalized communication, commerce, and science as well as the day to day life.
